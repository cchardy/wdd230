Rule of Thirds

When the viewer arrives at the Marriott website, the main image is a great example of the Rule of Thirds. The woman sitting relaxing outside her hotel room brings balance and vocus to the website.

Marriott

Visual Hierarchy

The Library of Congress's website shows the principle of visual hierarchy. The viewer sees the opening image with it's contrasting colors and then the eye travels to the image title. From there the eye focuses on the title of the website

Library of Congress

White Space and Clean Design

Magazzino is a beautiful example of White Space and a Clean Design. The image enhances the white space and gives a secondary attention to the typography. The font is a sans-serif which lends itself to a clean design.
